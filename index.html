Szachowa Vistula

"Szachowa Vistula Chess Monthly" appears on the network since December 1997. Publications include both current chess events, as well as historical, remote in time. The results of the competition chess, stock lot, articles on various subjects, all reader will find in the next editions of the monthly.

The current issue of the monthly magazine are available at szachowavistula.pl/vistula. In the main issue of the monthly reader will find a collection of essays devoted to the events and characters associated with the game of chess, publications containing the results of the competition and sets the party, messages announcing upcoming tournaments, the current FIDE rating, tasks chess and lots of other stuff. W głównym wydaniu miesięcznika czytelnik znajdzie zbiory felietonów poświęconych wydarzeniom i postaciom związanym z grą w szachy, publikacje zawierające wyniki zawodów oraz zbiory partii, komunikaty zapowiadające nadchodzące turnieje, aktualny ranking FIDE, zadania szachowe i wiele innych rzeczy.
